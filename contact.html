Newark travel Refund Policy

Our Refund Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your Newark experience. If you need to cancel your booking, here's our policy:

Cancellation Timeframes

  • 30+ days before departure: Full refund minus $50 processing fee
  • 15-29 days before departure: 75% refund
  • 8-14 days before departure: 50% refund
  • 7 days or less: No refund available

Special Circumstances

Full refunds will be granted for:

  • Documented medical emergencies
  • Military deployment
  • Natural disasters affecting travel
