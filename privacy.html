Newark Travel Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [March-02-2025]

This Privacy Policy describes how Newark Travel collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our services or interact with our website.

1. Information Collection

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information necessary to provide travel services, including:

  • Personal identification details (name, date of birth, passport information)
  • Contact information (email, phone number, address)
  • Payment and billing information
  • Travel preferences and special requirements

1.2 Automated Collection

Our systems automatically collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Website usage data through cookies

2. Use of Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and manage travel bookings
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services
  • Comply with legal requirements
  • Prevent fraudulent activity

3. Data Sharing

We share information with:

  • Travel service providers (hotels, airlines, etc.)
  • Payment processors and financial institutions
  • Government authorities when required by law
  • Professional advisors (lawyers, accountants)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures including:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure network infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments

5. Your Rights

You may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Receive your data in a portable format

6. International Transfers

As a global travel provider, your data may be transferred internationally. We ensure all transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through our website or directly to customers when required by law.
