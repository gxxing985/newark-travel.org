Curated Experiences Along the Route

Santa Barbara Mission

Step into California's colonial history at this 1786 Spanish mission. Wander through the "Queen of Missions" rose garden and admire the original Chumash artwork in the church. Our guided tour reveals untold stories of the mission system.

San Luis Obispo

Discover this charming town's historic adobes and the iconic Bubblegum Alley. The art deco train station features remarkable 1940s architecture. We'll take you to the hidden speakeasy-style café favored by locals.

Salinas Valley

Watch the "Salad Bowl of America" unfold as we pass endless fields of artichokes and strawberries. The train slows for perfect viewing of this agricultural wonder, with narration about Steinbeck's connection to the region.

Mount Shasta Vista

Marvel at the 14,179-foot stratovolcano dominating the landscape. On clear mornings, its snow-capped peak reflects perfectly in alpine lakes. Our guide explains the mountain's spiritual significance to Native tribes.

Eugene Arts District

Explore this creative hub's galleries and craft breweries during our extended stop. The historic 1912 train station showcases beautiful railroad architecture and houses a museum of Oregon rail history.

Columbia River Gorge

Wind through dramatic basalt cliffs where the river carved its path. The observation car provides perfect viewing of Multnomah Falls' 620-foot cascade. Learn about the geologic forces that shaped this landscape.
